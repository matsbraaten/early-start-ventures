About

Three decades at the intersection of markets and regulation

Early Start Ventures is the consulting and advisory practice of Torstein Braaten — built on the conviction that firms that engage regulation early gain a lasting advantage.

Torstein Braaten

Torstein Braaten is a compliance executive with more than thirty years of experience advising banks, investment dealers, and crypto platforms. His career spans the full arc of modern Canadian capital markets — from traditional wealth management and institutional trading to the front lines of digital asset regulation — giving him a rare, practitioner's view of how sound compliance is actually built and run.

Most recently, Torstein served as Chief Compliance Officer at WonderFi Technologies, one of Canada's leading digital asset companies. Before that he was Head of Regulatory Affairs and Chief Compliance Officer at Bitbuy, where he helped guide the platform's journey as a registered Canadian crypto-asset dealer and marketplace, and Chief Compliance Officer at Coinsquare. Together, these roles placed him at the centre of Canada's effort to bring crypto trading platforms inside the regulatory perimeter.

His digital asset work rests on a deep foundation in traditional finance. As Chief Operating and Compliance Officer of Instinet Canada from 2018 to 2021, he oversaw both the operations and the regulatory obligations of a global institutional broker's Canadian arm. From 2016 to 2018 he was Chief Compliance Officer at BMO Nesbitt Burns, one of Canada's largest full-service investment dealers.

Regulators have repeatedly sought out that experience. Torstein has served on the Ontario Securities Commission's Fintech Advisory Committee and on IIROC's Crypto-Asset Working Group, helping shape the frameworks that now govern digital asset markets in Canada.

He holds an Honours Bachelor of Commerce from Carleton University, the ICD.D designation from the Institute of Corporate Directors, and is a Certified Securities Compliance Professional.

The practice

Early Start Ventures is deliberately small: one senior practitioner, working directly with founders and leadership teams. The mission is simple — give early-stage companies, especially in fintech and digital assets, an early and informed start on compliance and regulatory strategy. Compliance is not a cost of doing business; it is how durable businesses are built. Every engagement pairs decades of hands-on experience in capital markets compliance with close attention to the rules as they are being written, so clients know what is coming, prepare before deadlines arrive, and grow with confidence.
